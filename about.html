About PIRCA

People In Resistance to Creeping Autocracy

Our Mission

PIRCA is a community-driven vigilance initiative committed to confronting creeping autocracy through digital forensics, narrative intelligence, and open-source resistance. We cut through the noise where systems of distortion prevail, track and map disinformation ecosystems, and defend truth in a polarized and collapsing information landscape.

What We Do

  • Monitor Systems of Distortion: Detect power-aligned disinfo using open-source analysis.
  • Equip the Public: Build awareness through accessible education and digital tools.
  • Defend Narrative Integrity: Archive, expose, and resist coordinated manipulation.

The DUB-O-TAKE Engine

At the heart of PIRCA is the DUB-O-TAKE Suite—a modular framework for fighting disinformation at scale. It includes signal analysis, narrative lineage tracking, and disinfo signature archiving.

Our Principles

  • Nonpartisan Vigilance
  • Collective Accountability
  • Transparent Technology
  • Narrative Immunity
  • Zero Tolerance for Authoritarian Creep

We are PIRCA. No slogan—just action.
